# Introduction to the Rust language

This is the MyST port of the Rust course originally authored in LaTeX by
Pierrick Roger.

The following parts have been ported so far:

- *Preliminaries*: chapters 1 (Introduction) and 2 (Environment).
- *Basics*: chapters 3 (Variables & constants) through 8 (Functions —
  Part I).
- *Fundamentals*: chapters 9 (Macros introduction) through 14 (Slices).
- *Proficiency*: chapters 15 (Functions — Part II) through 22
  (Testing).
- *Expertise*: chapters 23 (Traits) through 25 (Collections).
- *Appendices > Tables*: full chapter.
- *Appendices > Solutions*: *First program* (chapter 8) and *Nuts
  price* (chapter 12) so far.
