# Course readiness level

We use colored banners to warn that a section or a part
in the book is *not yet ready* for reading.
The level of readiness is indicated by the text and the color of the
banner. The different banners are presented here with their meanings.

:::{danger} Draft
This signals that the section is not in any readable state.
It contains ideas, draft texts and unchecked examples that almost
always fail.
It may also contains wrong statements.
:::

:::{danger} In progress
The section contains text in active state of writing or correction.
Some part of it may be readable, some not. Moreover it may lack some
or many important explanations on the treated subject.
:::

:::{danger} TODO
These banners simply list tasks or ideas for the current section.
:::

:::{warning} To review
The section is ready for a review by the author himself.
:::

:::{note} Finalized
The section is ready for a review by the proofreaders.
:::
